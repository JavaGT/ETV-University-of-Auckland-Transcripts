Humates for Improved Production Humates are organic acids derived from the outer layers of some coal seams. They are used to condition soils and increase plant growth. Mixed with conventional fertilisers they help retain P and N nutrients in the soil and act synergistically to make nutrients more available to plants. In NZ, humates are extracted at the New Vale coal mine near Gore, crushed and shipped around the country.
