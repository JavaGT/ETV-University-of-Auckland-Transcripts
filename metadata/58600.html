This video, from the Australian Bureau of Statistics website, is an example of this kind of graphic used to show population growth and decline. Dynamic population pyramids demonstrate this process in action, leading up to and beyond the point (different for each country) where natural increase (growth) shifts to become natural decrease (decline).
