Tiro whānui: Manawarū ana a Whakaata Māori ki te pāho i ngā take ka kawea mai e Mihingarangi Forbes, uri o Ngāti Maniapoto, Ngāti Paoa, ngā pūrongo o ngā take mohoa a Native Affairs. He kairipoata whai tohu a Mihingarangi. I toa a Native Affairs i ngā Whakawhiwhinga Tohu Kiriata/Whakaata o Aotearoa mō ngā take mohoa whaitake rawa atu i te tau 2011, he kaiwhakataetae hoki mō te Tohu o te Ao CBA-Amnesty mo te Hōtaka Mana Tangata i ngā tohu ā-ao a te Commonwealth Broadcasting Association, i tū ki Johannesburg. About the series: Māori Television's flagship current affairs show, Native Affairs, is presented by award-winning journalist Mihingarangi Forbes (Ngāti Maniapoto, Ngāti Paoa). The show's accolades include former presenter, Julian Wilcox, winning top honours at the 2012 New Zealand Television Awards for Best News or Current Affairs Presenter, and Best Presenter: Te Reo Māori for his live funeral coverage of the Venerable Dr Hone Kaa last April.
