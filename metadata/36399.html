The parade began on the corner of Rolleston Avenue at 3.30pm before moving to Park Terrace and finishing at the fanzone in north Hagley Park. French Rugby Players in altercation Police were called in to an incident involving players from the French rugby team in downtown Auckland. A photographer alleged he was spat on by French lock Pascal Pape, while another said he was slapped in the face and had his memory card taken by another player. A man who was dining at Pastis at the time of the altercation said the rugby players were having a good time, eating and drinking, when the photographer entered the restaurant and confronted them with his camera. Police were called about the incident but ONE News understands no charges have been laid.
