etv/supersizers_go_prime_nz_20130221_1930_640x360The Supersizers Go... - Edwardian SpecialDiet of the Edwardian period, with 5 meals a day, heavy in meat and pudding, which, on one day, sum up to 5,000 calories. With chef Sophie Grigson. Giles Coren and Sue Perkins spend the week on the diet of a wealthy Edwardian Couple. Cooking for them at home is best selling cookery writer Sophie Grigson. Enormous amounts of offal, game, red meats and eggs are consumed from breakfast to late night snacks. Over the week they have a 9 course dinner party with guest including Roy Hattersley, an authentic 1905 society banquet attended by Anton Mosimann at The Savoy, and go on the little known chew chew diet. Overwhelmed by the amounts they have to eat - over 5,000 calories a day - Sue and Giles knock back the alcohol, play practical jokes and find themselves constipated for most of the week.01:06:28DocumentaryFood Science, HistoryGSky Open2013-02-2119:30:00
