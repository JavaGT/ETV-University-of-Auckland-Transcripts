Alison’s husband Cole (Joshua Jackson) is struggling to keep it all together in the wake of tragedy, and with the financially-strained ranch that has been in his family for generations. Their emotionally-charged marriage becomes even more complicated as Alison’s affair with Noah escalates, in turn casting a shadow over Noah’s marriage to Helen (Maura Tierney), his college sweetheart, best friend, mother of his children, lover and wife of 17 years.
