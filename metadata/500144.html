pts/library_upload/2809_898200/Pictorial_Parade_No_99LIBRARY - Archive NZ - Pictorial Parade No 99(1960) Experimental Express: All aboard an express train from Wellington to Auckland to test the theory that the trip can take 11 hours, that's two and a half hours faster than the present journey. Amazing and varied footage of the train making the trip and countryside and town GVs throughout the central North Island. <br>003.35 Christchurch: New Airport Building. Lots of pomp and ceremony on the tarmac at Christchurch Airport as Governor General Lord Cobham opens the new airport building and air traffic control tower. Soldiers parade, brass bands play, a Teal jet arrives from Sydney. 04.45 World Champion Racing Driver Tries Jet Boat: Formula One world motor racing champion Jack Brabham visits New Zealand. Brabham races a Cooper Climax race car round the tarmac at Christchurch Airport. Brabham meets Bill Hamilton and tries out his Hamilton jet boat up the Waimakariri Gorge. Great GVs of the jet boat being put through its paces. 06.35 He Iwi Tahi Tauto Waitangi Day: Naval exercise GVs off Bay of Islands involving NZ and Australian navy frigates. Jets fly over. On board GVs including crew and machinery at work. Waitangi Treaty grounds GVs. Local schoolchildren get a trip round the bar in a navy boat. Regatta GVs in Bay of Islands. Governor General leads the evening ceremony at the Treaty Grounds GVs.
