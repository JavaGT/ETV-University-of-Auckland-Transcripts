etv/25664_pictorial parade n_640x360LIBRARY - Archive NZ - Pictorial Parade No 90 - 1959BIG GREY BRIDGE. 106 ft. On the West Coast, the Minister of Works opens a new bridge over the Grey River, replacing a fifty year old wooden bridge. THE LAST GOLD ASSAYER. 206 ft. In the vaults of a Dunedin bank Mr Ted Ashby, New Zealand's last gold assayer, follows his exacting trade - testing the value of gold. RIVERS OF POWER. 353 ft. An aerial trip up the Waikato River shows established dams and new ones under construction. In the South Island the massive Benmore scheme is under way, the first of a series of dams to be built in the Waitaki Valley. GVs Waikato River and Meremere Power Station – coal-fired electricity. Aerials of Waikato hydro schemes, Karapiro and Awapuni (the grandfather). New dam under construction at Waipapa. Aerials of Maraetai and Whakamaru. GVs Atiamuri. GVs new dam at Ohakuri under construction. Geothermal steam at Wairakei. Wide shots of Otematata near Benmore (some great shots of cascading water at the end) Duration 07.26G2014-05-2309:43:10
