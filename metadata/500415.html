pts/library_upload/7020_375130/understanding_nceaLIBRARY - NZQA - Understanding NCEA'Understanding NCEA' looks at the national qualification system for New Zealand's senior secondary school students. It includes interviews with students, parents and teachers as well as with John Hattie, Professor of Education at the University of Auckland. The history, developments and benefits of NCEA framework are described as it challenges students of all abilities in all learning areas, enabling students to gain credits from both traditional school curriculum areas and alternative programmes. The National Certificate of Educational Achievement (NCEA)is recognised by employers and is used as the benchmark for selection by universities and polytechnics and is readily accepted overseas, including by universities.
