pts/library_upload/2810_460063/Pictorial_Parade_No_101LIBRARY - Archive NZ - Pictorial Parade No 101(1960) USS Halibut Nuclear Submarine: The first nuclear powered vessel to visit New Zealand, GVs of the submarine in Wellington Harbour and tied up at dock. Minister of Defence visits. GVs inside submarine. Halibut was the first submarine designed to launch guided missiles. Intended to carry the Regulus nuclear missile, the sub's main deck was high above the waterline to provide a dry "flight deck." Her missile system was completely automated, with hydraulic machinery controlled from a central control station.
