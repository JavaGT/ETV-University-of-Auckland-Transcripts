etv/ilgk_zBu2Js_640x360ONLINE COLLECTIONS - TECH - Designing with Recycled Materials (Preview)Government legislation, consumer pressure and new technology are all creating opportunities for designers. This programme shows how Sony and Indesit are rising to the challenge by designing mass-produced products from recycled plastics. Case studies include a chair made from Playstations and washing machines that use parts made from recycled fridge plastic. We follow the design process from concept, to modelling, testing and evaluation. We also visit a state of the art plastics recycling plant to see how new technology is being used to process and convert waste products into high-grade polymers and how material properties and characteristics are identified using both destructive and non-destructive tests. The pros and cons of using virgin vs recycled materials are then discussed. 2009.
