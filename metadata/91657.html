Often, the lines of what is appropriate are blurred, but the staff seem to love their work environment because of it. He helps an administration assistant find a new boyfriend, organises blind dating evenings and plays a fair number of practical jokes. Meanwhile, the staff at The Call Centre also form their own story lines throughout the show.
