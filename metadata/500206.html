pts/library_upload/3862_482363/Weekly_Review_No_169LIBRARY - Archive NZ - Weekly Review No 169(1944) Arrivals from the Middle East, New Zealand Soldiers and Polish Children: Prime Minister Peter Fraser welcomes a ship home with New Zealand troops on furlough and 838 Polish refugees who have come to New Zealand for the duration of the war. The refugees, most of them children, travel by train to their new homes at a camp at Paihiatua. All along the way they are welcomed by waving crowds of well-wishers.
