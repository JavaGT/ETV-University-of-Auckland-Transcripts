For ten years, Ozzy was happy to feed the myth of the Rock n Roll Wildman, living a life that was extreme even by the most bacchanalian standards of his chosen profession. The life worked for a while, but then it began to back-fire. He lost his family, his wife, even his livelihood when Black Sabbath fired him. The glamour had been leached away and the fun had faded. Featuring interviews with his brothers and sisters, as well as Jack, Sharon, Aimee and Kelly Osbourne, God Bless Ozzy Osbourne is about the intoxicating rush of success and the redemption that comes from pulling away from the very thing that made you successful.
