pts/library_upload/4807_167595/Weekly_Review_No_238LIBRARY - Archive NZ - Weekly Review No 238(1946) Waitangi New Flagstaff: A new kauri flagpole is pit-sawn to replace the existing Treaty Grounds flagpole that is now unsound. 01.50 Sheep Farming, Power-Spray Dipping: At the Ruakura Agricultural Research Centre, experiments are being conducted with an Australian-built power spray to replace the traditional ponds or pits for sheep dipping to kill ticks and lice. 03.10 Playground, National Swimming Champs at Napier: A harbour swim in Napier starts the first national swimming championships for six years. At the Municipal Baths, the best swimmers and divers in New Zealand compete in their speciality events and at the Napier Soundshell, musical entertainment is put on. On Marine Parade, displays of elegant and comedic roller skating are put on.
