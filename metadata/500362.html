pts/library_upload/2249_525016/drying_wood_betterLIBRARY - E=MC2 - Drying Wood More EffectivelyThe Energy Management group in the Physics Department at Otago University carries out research into efficient energy use. Its activities are split into a study on industrial drying and one on improving energy efficiency in houses. <br> Because industrial drying is required for many products (e.g. vegetables, timber, sewage slurry, dairy products, meat waste) it places high demand on energy sources.
