Armstrong: The funeral of Hubert (Tim) Armstrong, labourer, miner, trade unionist and politician. Armstrong was one of the first parliamentary candidates to stand for the Labour Party and held the seat of Christchurch East from 1922 until his death. Armstrong introduced some historic industrial legislation including the beginning of the 40 hour week and compulsory trade union membership. 02.20 New Zealand Desert Gunners: New Zealand artillery soliders are outgunning German troops in North Africa.
