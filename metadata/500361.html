Traditionally NMR/MRI is performed using laboratory or clinic based superconducting magnets, but now it is moving out into industry in the form of portable permanent magnet-based systems. <br> NMR development is being driven by advances in electronics, computing and magnet technology and so continues to advance in capability and application.
