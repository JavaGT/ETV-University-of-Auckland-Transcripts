pts/library_upload/4801_724036/Weekly_Review_No_287LIBRARY - Archive NZ - Weekly Review No 287(1947) Wellington, Athletic Meet: Before a crowd of 6000 at Athletic Park, American athlete Al Hershey demonstrates his prowess with the discus and shot put and New Zealand champion Dough Harris trying to set a world record in the 880 yard race. 02.10 Wellington, Summer Fire: A blaze on the slopes of Mt Victoria above Wellington destroys bush and threatens houses. Firefighters drag hoses through thick undergrowth to fight the seat of the fire. 03.30 Prime Minister and Archbishop Panico Visit Polish Camp: Prime Minister Peter Fraser and Archbishop Panico visit the Polish childrens' refugee camp at Pahiatua. The children turn out in national costume and entertain the visitors with traditional dances. 04.45 Auckland Regatta Day: The 97th Auckland yachting regatta attracts thousands of spectators and hundreds of boats to the Waitemata Harbour. The A-class keelers race over 26 miles. P class yachts set their small spinnakers as the light breeze freshens. The Tauranga class yachtsmen practice capsizing their craft.
