He’s confident when speaking te reo Māori but he’s also on his own te ao Māori journey alongside the rest of his Spark whānau. Yet when a call comes in for him to lead a mihi whakatau in the large atrium of Spark’s Tāmaki Makaurau HQ, Riki still gets nervous. Even as a confident speaker, standing up and speaking te reo in front of a group is, well, kinda scary.
