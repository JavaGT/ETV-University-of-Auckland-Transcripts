pts/library_upload/3341_504820/Pictorial_Parade_No_39LIBRARY - Archive NZ - Pictorial Parade No 39(1955) Look Before You Build, Models and Architects: A view of a new block of flats in Wellington turns out to be a scale model made by Ben Van Dorsselaer, Dutch-trained model maker in the Government Architect's office. The item shows some of the ways in which models are of great value in building projects. 02.20 New Canoe Sports, Slalom in the Rapids: After canoe racing on the Wanganui River at Aramoho, we go to the Manawatu Gorge for a demonstration of canoe slalom racing in the tumbling waters of the White Horse Rapids. 04.45 Hydro Electric Speed Up: The Load Despatcher room at Hamilton and heavy equipment for dam construction hurrying through the dusk from one site on the Waikato River to another as jobs are completed. At Whakamaru the power house is going up and the dam is nearing its full height.
