'The Story of India' is a BBC documentary series, written and presented by historian Michael Wood, about the history of India. It was originally aired on BBC Two in six episodes in August and September 2007 to mark the 60 years independence of India and Pakistan. An accompanying text to the series, titled 'Michael Wood: The Story of India', was published by BBC Books on 16 August 2007.
