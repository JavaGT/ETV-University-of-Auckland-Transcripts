The main purpose of the computer is processing of the payroll for 34,000 public servants. 01.45 Trim Models: The Otago Model Engineering Society turns on a fascinating display of railway trains, traction engines, ships, submarines, and radio-controlled boats for the Society's 25th Anniversary. 04.00 Golden Shears: For the first time, the world's top shearers meet, at Masterton, in open competition, and Ivan Bowen wins the International Championship. 06.55 Tobacco: Under the Nelson sun, the annual tobacco crop is harvested by machine.
