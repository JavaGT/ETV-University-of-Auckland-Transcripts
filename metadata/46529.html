'Wallander' (Swedish pronunciation: [valˈlanːdər]) is a Swedish television series adapted from Henning Mankell's Kurt 'Wallander' novels and starring Krister Henriksson in the title role. The stories are set in Ystad, Scania near the southern tip of Sweden. The three films 'Before the Frost', 'Mastermind', and 'The Secret' were premiered in cinemas, with the rest first released as direct-to-DVD movies. The first episode of the second series, 'Hämnden' (The Revenge), was released in Swedish cinemas in January 2009; the rest of the series was made for television. A third and final season, containing six 90 minute episodes, aired in 2013 with Charlotta Jonsson as Linda Wallander.
