etv/25671_weekly_review_no_202_640x360LIBRARY - Archive NZ - Weekly Review No 202 - 1945Wellington. Presentation of Freyberg Flag. Communications. New Makara Radio. Highbank. Largest power unit in N.Z. in operation. At the source of the Rangitata Rivers is the beginning of one of the biggest public work schemes undertaken in NZ – the Rangitata Diversion to irrigate a large part of mid-Canterbury and to use the surplus water for the Highgate Power Station on the Rakaia River. Equipment for the power station coming from England was lost at sea. GVs of the turning on ceremony with Minister of Works Bob Semple, great shot of dignitaries standing on pipes spewing out racing water. Duration 08.1100:08:10G2014-05-2311:32:01
