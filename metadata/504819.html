etv/market_segmentation_20221011_1403Market SegmentationHow to Use Market Segmentation: Developing a Target Market Market segmentation is a commonly utilized concept in business where a larger market of consumers is divided into smaller groups of consumers who share certain characteristics. The goal is to generate what is known as a target market, which is an identifiable group of consumers who behave in a similar way, have similar interests, and share similar characteristics. Firms utilize demographic, geographic, psychographic, and behavioral segmentation as a way of generating a target market.
