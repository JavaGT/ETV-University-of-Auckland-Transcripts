etv/xGBekCxQk94_640x360ONLINE COLLECTIONS - SOCIAL - The Changing Role of Women in the UK (excerpt)Changing role of women in the UK since 1900. The 20th Century saw enormous change for women in the home, at work and in social values. Even with legislation to ensure equal pay for equal work; sexual discrimination outlawed and a female former Prime Minister we still ask, Are women equal to men? Today there is almost an expectation that women will work, as well as take care of the cooking, cleaning and family duties. What has brought about these changes, and are they changes for the better? Explore these issues further with this brand new programme, made with the help of the National Subject Leader for History and exclusive to Classroom Video Ltd. An excellent resource when teaching History at Key Stage 3 and also Modern World at GCSE.
