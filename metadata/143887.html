Having turned his own life around, he wants to give the First Nations community something millions of other Canadians take for granted: the internet. Convinced that connectivity will open up new job and education opportunities, Bruce sets about building Maskwacis its own high-speed internet network. But with no backing, piling debt and huge technical obstacles, will Bruce succeed in bringing Maskwacis into the digital age?
