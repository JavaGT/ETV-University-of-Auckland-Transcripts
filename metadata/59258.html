Semi and permanent hair colour applications can vary from manufacturer to manufacturer and it is important to follow the precise instructions given. It is also very important to perform a skin test prior to applying any colour to the hair, to avoid any possible allergic reactions. This film is full of handy tips to help students complete colouring successfully and safely.
