etv/4ovhEkSIqV0_640x360ONLINE COLLECTIONS - SCIENCE - MIT Physics Demo -- Low Friction Atwood MachineA string carrying two weights is hung over a low friction bearing mounted pulley. The weights have slightly different masses, causing a uniform acceleration. When the time it takes the weights to move 1 meter is timed, we can calculate the acceleration of the system due to gravity. Because of the low amount of friction in the system, this value is very close to the theoretical value.
