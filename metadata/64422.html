etv/_mCC-68LyZM_640x360ONLINE COLLECTIONS - SCIENCE - Misconceptions About Falling ObjectsIf you drop a heavy object and a light object simultaneously, which one will reach the ground first? A lot of people will say the heavy object, but what about those who know both will land at the same time? What do they think? Some believe both objects have the same gravitational pull on them and/or both fall to the ground with the same constant speed. Neither of these things is true, however.
