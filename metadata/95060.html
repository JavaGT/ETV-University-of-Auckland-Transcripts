Presented by Paraone Gloyne, Taniwha Rau explores another tribal region; Tainui, and features the characteristics unique to this tribal dialect. Featuring those kaumātua who are so far off the beaten track, so immersed in their own communities and their own ‘kōwhao’, that many viewers will be meeting them for the first time.
