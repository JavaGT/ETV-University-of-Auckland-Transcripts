Instead of Catalonians voting on independence from Spain on November 9, they will now simply participate in a non-binding symbolic poll. Regional leader Artur Mas watered down the planned referendum after the Spanish government challenged its legality. Now with thousands angered over what’s left of the referendum, disharmony in the Catalan leadership is growing. So, what lies ahead for the separatist movement?
