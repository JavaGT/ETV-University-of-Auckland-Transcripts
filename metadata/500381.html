In 1939, the Battle of the River Plate was the first time the RNZN ensign was flown in action when the destroyers Exeter, Ajax and Achilles took on the 11" guns of the German pocket battleship Graf Spree off Montevideo. Eddie Telford was aboard the Achilles and gives a firsthand account of the battle which resulted in the sinking of the Graf Spree.
