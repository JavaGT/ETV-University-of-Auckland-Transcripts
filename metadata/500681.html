Student Learning Drop Cover Hold Earthquakes in New Zealand - Earthquakes in New Zealand. Student Learning &quot;Drop, Cover, Hold&quot;This was an intermediate student led project, it focuses on the impact that earthquakes have on New Zealand, all of the scripts and filming was facilitated by the students. This film has a number of Enhanced Video Annotations (EVA) which allows students to enjoy a safe interactive experience either in the classroom or at home.
