About Seven Sharp: Seven Sharp is a half hour long New Zealand current affairs programme produced by Television New Zealand. The programme presents up to 8 stories within a 30 minute timeslot every week night and is integrated with social media and real time opinions. From the Seven Sharp website: Our goal is to keep you informed, discuss what you're discussing but never take ourselves too seriously. We want to celebrate being Kiwi, and not just have a doom and gloom look at the world. This country is brimming with talent and characters and we want to find the stories around them.
