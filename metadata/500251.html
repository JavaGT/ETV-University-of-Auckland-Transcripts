pts/library_upload/4786_824339/Weekly_Review_No_223LIBRARY - Archive NZ - Weekly Review No 223(1945) Soviet Minister to New Zealand, Mr Ivan K Ziabkin: The arrive of the the first diplomatic representative to New Zealand from the Soviet Union. The party's first visit is to the Savage Memorial on Auckland's waterfront to pay a tribute to the late New Zealand prime minister. 02.00 HMS Indefatigable: Big British Carrier's Arrival: The aircraft carrier Indefatigable enters Wellington Harbour and ties up at Port Nicholson - the first fleet carrier to visit New Zealand waters. The NZ army has constructed a special gangway out of Bailey bridge sections. 03.15 Auckland, A Chinese Play: A modern Chinese play called "Human Integrity" is performed in Auckland.
