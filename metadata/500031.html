pts/library_upload/6013_187785/Pictorial_Parade_No_189LIBRARY - Archive NZ - Pictorial Parade No 189(1967) Sailing Z Class: The annual Cornwall Cup competition is raced on Paremata Harbour. All the sailors in each two-person crew are under the age of 19. The boats are Z Class - 12 and a half feet long and five feet wide and are identical in every curve and plane. 03.00 Bands Disband: The National Band of New Zealand goes on a national tour just before temporarily disbanding. The band gives a demonstration of music and precision marching. 05.40 Those Magnificent Venturers: At Matamata Aerodrom, 38 Venturer scouts are on a week-long summer camp to take to the air. It's a flying school for 15 to 18 year olds where the trainers are experienced pilots, including some from Air New Zealand.
