etv/legends_of_opera_arts_channel_20190621_1945_640x360Legends of Opera - Season 1 - Episode 1, Luciano PavarottiThe first of a new series looking at great singers also launches a night of programming on Pavarotti. If you feel it’s well-trodden ground, you may be surprised by some of the archive clips up the programme’s sleeve. There are sweet stills of “Big Lucy” as a boy and of his teenage period as a would-be footballer in Modena, plus some great early video from Italian television in the 1960s, though sadly, no clips remain of his introduction to British viewers on Sunday Night at the London Palladium. Among the talking heads singing his praises are critic Rupert Christiansen and Classic FM broadcaster Rob Cowan, who describes his voice as “molten gold, pure beauty”. About series: This series travels to the great opera houses of the world, from Milan and Venice, to London and New York, to pay tribute to the greatest opera singers of our time. Hosted and narrated by operatic soprano, Danielle de Niese. Featuring classic moments in the careers of twelve legends of opera, from Luciano Pavarotti to Enrico Caruso, Joan Sutherland to Kiri Te Kanawa and Jessye Norman to Mario Lanza.
