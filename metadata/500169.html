pts/library_upload/3646_697133/Weekly_Review_No_215LIBRARY - Archive NZ - Weekly Review No 215(1945) New Zealand Cameraman in Singapore: A newsreel cameraman records the scenes as the RNZAF evacuates New Zealand prisoners of war from Japanese prison camps in Singapore. There is film inside the notorious Changi Jail, built to house 620 inmates but crowded with 5300 men at the height of the war. The cameras follow the former POWs on their way home as the RNZAF planes travel back via Indonesia and Siam (Thailand) to pick up more New Zealanders. The film ends with Dr John, a former POW in Singapore, saying that reports of Japanese atrocities should not be discounted as he has firsthand knowledge that they are accurate.00:08:52Short FilmHistory, New ZealandG2010-01-2000:00:00
