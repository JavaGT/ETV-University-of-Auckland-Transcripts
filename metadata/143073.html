They play a few songs from their new album, Sky Is Falling. The members of Ghost Town include veteran New Zealand rocker Jed Town (The Features, Fetus Productions, ICU), Mark Beesley (Dean Savage) and drummer Bryan Shaw along with Dominic Blaazer (keys) and Steven Shaw (bass). About the Series: New Zealand Music Month is celebrated in May. It’s May the 1st 2017 and that means NZ Music Month is officially underway – New Zealand’s annual celebration of homegrown music and the people who make it! It’s 31 days of celebrating local music in our venues, schools, libraries, parks, on our screens and on our stereos. With over 800 events around the country listed on our gig guide already, this year’s celebrations are busier than ever.
