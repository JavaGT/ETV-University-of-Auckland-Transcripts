pts/library_upload/4139_319369/Weekly_Review_No_83LIBRARY - Archive NZ - Weekly Review No 83(1943) Airforce on Parade in Nelson: Air Commodore Vincent inspects RNZAF men at a Nelson air station as part of an open day to raise Patriotic Funds. The airman stage a massed march past and boys of the Air Training Corp perform a gynmastics display. Also on parade are Red Cross nurses and members of the Womens War Service Axillary. The highlight of the day is a spectacular ceremony based on the Trooping of the Colours. 01.55 Armed Forces Services Club: The Governor General takes the salute at the entrance to what was once Government House in Auckland - now the Armed Forces Services Club. Servicemen and women and members of the public relax in the grounds and inside the historic house which has been renovated for its new purpose. 03.00 Suva Market: A tour around Suva Market in Fiji as New Zealand soldiers shop for souvenirs.00:04:08Short FilmHistory, New Zealand, SportG2010-01-2000:00:00
