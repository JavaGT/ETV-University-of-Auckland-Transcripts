FLOODS IN WAIRARAPA 101 ft. 03.35 ELECTRIC POWER.... CRISIS IN SOUTH ISLAND Lake Coleridge Power Station GVs, drastic power cuts imposed over most of South Island because of low lake and river levels. Dry autumn also impacts of Lake Tekapo and Lake Pukaki and Ohau. Talks about scheme to raise level of Tekapo by 15 feet. Lake outflow will be piped through a 5000 ft tunnel. GVs of tunnel being built. Housing shortage in this remote location. Housing GVs – could be used generically. GVs house building. Pukati development – outflow is diverted through five sluices and lake level goes up 18 ft. This will allow more water to be stored. Duration 8.05G2014-05-2312:10:35
