Nanotechnology is a new technology based on the manipulation of individual atoms to form structures and devices that have new and unusual different properties. <br> IRL has worked for several years with a Christchurch-based company, Nanocluster Devices Ltd, which builds devices such as nanowires from nanoparticles.
