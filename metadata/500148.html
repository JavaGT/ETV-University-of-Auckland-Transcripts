Rotorua sawmill GVs. The 4-masted sailing ship Pamir GVs at sea. 02.20 The War in Africa is over and troops from the New Zealand Division are on the long journey back to their base in Cairo, Eygpt. A convoy of 2000 vehicles travels along the desert road with airforce protection overhead.
