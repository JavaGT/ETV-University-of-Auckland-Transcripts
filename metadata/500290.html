pts/library_upload/6692_735653/GallacherLIBRARY - Archive NZ - Moments in History: Gallaher - GallaherThis film clip celebrates the return of the victorious All Blacks from their tour of the British Isles, France and the United States of America during 1905 and 1906. The film briefly describes a celebratory dinner held in honour of the returning rugby team before focusing on the life of team captain, Dave Gallagher. The film shows that Gallaher served as a New Zealand soldier in the South African War in 1901 and following his rugby success, went on to serve in the First World War where he was killed in action.
