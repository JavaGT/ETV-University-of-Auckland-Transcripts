pts/library_upload/3790_440521/Weekly_Review_No_371LIBRARY - Archive NZ - Weekly Review No 371(1948) Scene Shifting, Old Vic Air Lift: On its tour of New Zealand, the Old Vic Theatrical Company uses five aircraft to transport its actors and stage props and scenery for its production of A School for Scandal. The stars, Sir Laurence Olivier and Vivian Leigh, arrive at Paraparaumu Airport. 02.35 Mangere Air Display: To commemorate the eighth anniversary of the Battle of Britain, an air pageant is held in Auckland - the first in New Zealand since 1939. Historic airplanes chase balloons and try to burst them. Pilots try dead-stick landings - without engines or propellers. 03.55 Rugby, North versus South: The inter-island rugby match at Athletic Park in Wellington features many players in line for next year's tour to South Africa.
