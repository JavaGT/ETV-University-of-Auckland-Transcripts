Presenter Russell Brown and the rest of the Media3 team believe in being both intelligent and entertaining -- and creating more light than heat. In a media sector experiencing rapid and historic change -- not least in the blurring of the lines between consumers and producers of media -- there'll never be a shortage of stories.
