Children being taught to swim and the annual surf championships at Lyall Bay, Wellington. RODEO AT STRATFORD. 260 ft. Thrills and spills at a New Zealand championship rodeo, where horse meets rider, and rider meets the ground.  COAL STEAM PLANT BEING BUILT. 257 ft. Preliminary work on the building of the coal-electric powerhouse at Mercer, and the removal of overburden from the opencast coal field at Maramarua. At about 8.30, VO says the new power station will generate twice as much as Karapiro. “To keep up with the country’s demand for more and more electricity, all available sources are being harnessed.” Two thirds of the coal comes from nearby Maramarua.
