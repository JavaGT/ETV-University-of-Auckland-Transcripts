pts/library_upload/3365_553096/Pictorial_Parade_No_54LIBRARY - Archive NZ - Pictorial Parade No 54(1956) RAF Vulcan Visits Ohakea: The Avro Vulcan bomber flies over Wellington and lands at Ohakea. Crowds see the plane before it takes off and we say good-bye as the Vulcan flies past Mount Egmont. Tragically, the bomber crashed on landing back in London, killing four of the crew on board. 02.40: Medical Airlift from the Tokelau Islands: An extra polio ward is completed in Samoa, while nurses are flown north to the Tokelau Islands to bring back patients. Physiotherapist Miss Wilson, of Wellington Hospital, attends to patients on the islands and supervises their transfer to Apia Hospital for specialist treatment. 05.25 Jungle Patrol by SAS Squadron: At Kuala Lumpur the C.O. of the New Zealand S.A.S. Squadron briefs Corporal Huia Woods for a jungle patrol.
