etv/KxHJB0Y31zk_640x360ONLINE COLLECTIONS - MATHS - Commuter View: Visualising commuter flows in New ZealandThe 2006 New Zealand Census of Population and Dwellings contained questions, 'Where do you live?' and 'Where do you work?', that can be used to calculate commuter flows. However, even at the highest level of geography tables of these flows would be very large (over 5,000 cells). By providing this data on a map this massive table can be reduced, viewed and used for planning purposes. The 'Commuterview' tool demonstrated in this video is available on request from Statistics New Zealand. It uses 'spider' graphs to show commuter flows. These can be defined for selected ethnic groups, modes of transport, industry and occupation.00:07:17DocumentaryHistoryPGR2013-07-0200:00:00
