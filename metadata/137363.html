Belief examines the 2007 death of young Wainuiomata mother Janet Moses during an attempted exorcism. The film uses a blend of interviews and reenactments to explore a tragedy which director David Stubbs believes was caused by "fear and love mixing and turning into hysteria". The case was reported around the world. In 2009 five members of Janet’s close family were charged in relation to her death. David Stubbs, himself from the isolated Wellington suburb, has re-told those events with a nuanced, careful film - which had it's first television airing as part of Sunday Theatre - which he entitled Belief because he said it portrayed people who genuinely believed they were doing the right thing.01:34:13DocumentaryMaori, Media Studies, New Zealand, Psychology, Religion & TheologyAOTV 12016-10-1620:30:00
