Firstly an analysis has been conducted to map what proportional breakdown exists within the Vocational Pathways when viewed across the Learning Areas. Leaver data has also been mapped alongside the Vocational Pathways to identify what pathways leavers follow. A detailed analysis of what sector related standards her school offers across the Learning Areas is outlined. Research has been done on job prospects nationally, again mapped against the Vocational Pathways. This has then been repeated at a regional level using data for Taranaki.
