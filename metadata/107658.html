etv/te_karere_tv_one_20150630_1600_640x360Te Karere - 30 June 2015'Te Karere' is TVNZ's Māori news programme that broadcasts entirely in Māori. Presented by Scotty Morrison. Te Karere is a long-running daily news programme in te reo Māori. Based in the TVNZ newsroom, Te Karere covers key events and stories in the Māori world as well as bringing a Māori perspective to the day's news. Significant for pioneering Māori news on mainstream TV, for two decades it has been a platform for Māori to comment on issues and events.
