etv/miracle_in_a_box_a_p_the_arts_channel_20120220_1930_640x360Miracle in a Box: A Piano RebornDirected by Oscar-winning filmmaker John Korty, 'Miracle in a Box: A Piano Reborn' is an inspiring film about the coming together of three events: the bequest of a grand piano, the student competition to win it, and the months of work required to make it sing again. Callahan Piano artisans take apart and rebuild the instrument for the First Berkeley Piano Competition before our eyes, hewing to standards as precise as one-tenth of a gram. The close relationships and easy humor of the workshop accompany the highly professional techniques and tools. The winner of the competition, Jared Redmond, finally makes his debut on what is now his Steinway grand at the party given in the workshop as a finale.
