In a world of towering cliffs, white-sand beaches and cold clear seas lives an unforgettable cast of wild animals - otters, eagles and red deer, seals, dolphins and basking sharks. But living here isn't easy. Battered by a wild ocean and some of Europe's most unpredictable weather, the only certainty here is constant change.
