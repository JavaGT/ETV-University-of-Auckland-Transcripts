About series: If you are a fan of real crime ‘Whodunnits’ and CSI’s forensic science, then this new five-part local series will have you clinging to the edge of your seat. But be warned – sometimes the truth can be stranger than fiction. Produced in collaboration with the New Zealand Institute of Environmental Science and Research (ESR) and the NZ Police, this prestige new documentary series puts science to work to discover the truth behind real crime case files. Each episode focuses on a real, recent New Zealand crime case and the series as a whole will cover all aspects of forensic science from blood spatter, DNA, tool marking, digital forensics, toxicology to entomology and more. Host Simon Morton guides viewers on each fascinating journey, bringing together those involved from investigators to witnesses, helping viewers to piece together a trail of evidence showing how science proved the truth and solved the crime.
