Opening Day of the great Roxburgh hydro-electric station brought many gold-panners to the Clutha River for the few hours that the river level was lowered while a new lake filled behind the dam. Also shown are the gates closing at midnight, the waters rising and demolishing the coffer dam, the first flow over the spillways, and the starting up of the first generator. Duration 11.0000:10:33G2014-05-2213:01:35
