Holidays are celebrated in many ways, but chances are they involve eating, and eating a lot. Ever wonder why we cook our food? We do it because it tastes good, of course, and because our customs and traditions are built around it. But we also cook our food for some basic biological reasons. Some scientists think that working out how to cook actually made us human.
